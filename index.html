✨ New Products & Strategic Implementations

SuperTeacher TV App

Develop a lightweight, optimized app for low-powered school TVs, distributed via Play Store.

Devs: 2 ETA: 2 Months

SuperTeacher TV App

Description: Most schools have low-powered TVs, and because of this, the SuperTeacher app is not working well. Also, we are sharing TV APKs via Google Drive instead of the Play Store. Because of this, people are using the wrong APKs also not updating the apps to the latest versions, which is again hard and involves someone to monitor the Google Drive link. Instead, we can create a lightweight SuperTeacher app for TV that has only Standard Subject page, block list page, and the projector mode (No Teacher prep mode). We can host this app in the Play Store itself so that users can directly download from the Play Store and update via the Play Store itself

Prerequisites: Need design support for making the UI TV-friendly

This could increase the app engagement
